Welcome to Kingstown Turkish Barber

Best Turkish Barber Shop

Where tradition meets modern grooming in the heart of Dun Laoghaire. Nestled in Ireland’s best vibrant coastal town, our Turkish barber shop offers an authentic Turkish barbering experience infused with the warmth of Turkish hospitality. With skilled barbers trained in time-honoured techniques, we specialise in providing precision haircuts, traditional hot towel shaves, beard trims, and grooming services tailored to every client’s unique style.
